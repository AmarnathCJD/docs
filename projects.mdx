## Community Showcase

Discover amazing projects built by the Gogram community. These projects demonstrate the power and flexibility of Gogram for building Telegram bots and automation tools.

---

### Julia
[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Repository-181717?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/amarnathcjd/Julia)

Advanced bot with utility features, modular design, and extensive plugin support. Built for scalability and ease of customization.

---

### TgMusicBot
[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Repository-181717?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/AshokShau/TgMusicBot)

A high-performance Telegram bot for streaming music and video in voice chats, featuring playlist management and multi-language support.
